Esperanto

I speak Esperanto, and I currently serve as the president of the German Esperanto Youth (Deutsche Esperanto-Jugend), a youth organization dedicated to the promotion of the constructed auxiliary language Esperanto and, more generally, intercultural respect and understanding.

You can learn Esperanto on Duolingo or other platforms like Lernu.net. Fun fact: Esperanto has native speakers.

In the past, I have given online talks in Esperanto on a range of popular-scientific or social topics. Some have been recorded and can be accessed on YouTube:
